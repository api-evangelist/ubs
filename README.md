## UBS (ubs)

A Swiss multinational investment bank and financial services company and the world's largest wealth manager. UBS exposes a multi-ecosystem developer platform at developer.ubs.com covering retail banking, mortgages, QR billing, EU PSD2 open banking, Swiss TWINT payments, corporate bLink connectivity, and the enterprise UBS Partner / KeyPort offering.

**URL:** [https://www.ubs.com/](https://www.ubs.com/)

**Developer Portal:** [https://developer.ubs.com/](https://developer.ubs.com/)
